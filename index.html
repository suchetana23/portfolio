My Skills

Front-End Design

During my college journey, I delved into the realm of front-end designing through various projects. Utilizing HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I crafted an intuitive user interface that seamlessly adapted to different screen sizes. Implementing modern design principles and UI/UX best practices, the website not only enhanced the organization's online presence but also provided a user-friendly experience for visitors. Additionally, I explored frameworks such as Bootstrap and experimented with animations to elevate the aesthetics and functionality of the website.

AI-ML

As a student passionate about artificial intelligence and machine learning, I embarked on a captivating journey of exploration and discovery. Leveraging Python libraries such as TensorFlow and scikit-learn, I trained a machine learning algorithm to analyze user sentiments expressed in tweets. By preprocessing the textual data, engineering relevant features, and fine-tuning the model parameters, I achieved remarkable accuracy in predicting sentiment polarity. This project not only honed my skills in natural language processing but also provided valuable insights into the application of AI in social media analytics.

Cyber Security

In the realm of cyber security, I undertook a comprehensive research paper exploring the intricacies of network intrusion detection systems (NIDS). Investigating various NIDS architectures and detection techniques, I conducted a comparative analysis to evaluate their effectiveness in mitigating cyber threats. Drawing upon real-world case studies and simulation experiments, I assessed the strengths and limitations of different NIDS approaches in detecting and preventing network attacks.

Data Analytics

In the domain of data analytics, I spearheaded a project focusing on predictive analytics for customer churn in a telecommunications company. Employing techniques such as logistic regression and decision trees, I analyzed historical customer data to identify key factors influencing churn behavior. By developing predictive models and performing rigorous validation tests, I generated actionable insights to help the company proactively address customer attrition. Furthermore, I utilized data visualization tools such as Tableau to present the findings in a visually compelling manner, facilitating data-driven decision-making for the organization.

Business Analytics

In the realm of business analytics, I collaborated on a consulting project aimed at optimizing supply chain operations for a retail conglomerate. Employing statistical analysis and optimization algorithms, we analyzed vast datasets encompassing inventory levels, production schedules, and transportation logistics. By applying forecasting models and inventory optimization techniques, we identified opportunities to streamline procurement processes and minimize operational costs. Our recommendations, grounded in data-driven insights, empowered the client to make informed decisions and achieve tangible improvements in supply chain efficiency.

Video Editing

Venturing into the realm of video editing, I embarked on a creative project to produce a promotional video for a local charity event. Armed with Adobe Premiere Pro and After Effects, I meticulously curated footage, added visual effects, and synchronized audio elements to craft a compelling narrative. From storyboard conceptualization to final editing, I employed storytelling techniques and cinematic principles to captivate the audience's attention and evoke emotional resonance. The resulting video not only raised awareness about the charity event but also showcased my proficiency in video editing and storytelling.
